About Us

Anyas Burobanglid Farm Mountain Resort, located in Sebaste, Antique, Philippines, offers guests a serene retreat amidst lush greenery and natural landscapes. Our resort features flowing pools fed by natural springs, providing a refreshing experience for visitors seeking relaxation and a touch of nature. Guests can enjoy the tranquil environment, listen to the melodious sounds of birds, and immerse themselves in the beauty of the surrounding hills. Our accommodations are designed to provide comfort while harmonizing with the natural environment, ensuring a peaceful stay for all our visitors.
